Safe California

Winner of the 2017 AngelHack Silicon Valley National Environmental Health Association Challenge

An application that reports environmental data from CalEnviroScreen to help Californians stay informed on their environmental surroundings. Built in less than 24-hours.

Technology

Safe California is built on a React + Redux frontend coupled with a Node + Express backend with a PostgreSQL database. Safe California also utilizes Bootstrap 4 and MDBootstrap for a responsive, mobile-first layout experience.
